Learning to play Pong with Reinforcement learning

A project which demonstrate how Deep-Q-Networks (DQN) can be applied to solve two Reinforcement Learning (RL) problems. The first scenario is the reputed Cartpole problem, i.e. the Inverted Pendulum problem, where the task is to learn an agent to balance a cartpole by taking appropriate actions at certain states. The second scenario is to learn an agent to play the Atari game Pong, meaning that the agent should know when to take either action up or down in order to hit the ball, with the goal of winning over its opponent.
